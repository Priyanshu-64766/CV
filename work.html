I'm Lost

I sat down one day with a restless heart
Seemed like I’m missing out on a part
A part of my soul that feels long gone
Like a darkness devouring it on and on
It’s getting hard to keep it up anymore
It feels like nothing but I’m not so sure.

I’m trying and trying but it’s all in vain
Can’t hold it any longer against the pain
Sleepless nights and endless thoughts
Everything here has countless knots
Unconsciously trapped in my mind snares
But couldn’t scream ‘cause no one cares.

But I often think “Hey! What goes?”
Growing up’s a pain it’s just another dose
Then I pickup myself and gather all I got
I feel better or that’s what I thought
Dragging myself through this nasty path
Knowing deep down would lead to scathe.

I faced the outcome that I was doomed to
Was dispatched the darkness that was due
Humiliated and broken, I suffer all again
Out of breath going all insane.
